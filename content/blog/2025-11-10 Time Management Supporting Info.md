# Extra resources

During the podcast I mention two things that you may be interested in. The Eisenhower Matrix, and my Leader's week template.

## Usage

Every week on a Monday, I fill this in.
I use it to help indicate what I should be thinking about, and where I should be investing my time.

It also helps very clearly show if you have too many big tasks on.

I liberally splash emojis and icons throughout my document to give it a bit of life - I've got icons for different business units, and for certain types of task.
